This Will Destroy You is an American instrumental band known for its expansive, cinematic soundscapes that blur the lines between post-rock, ambient, and doomgaze. Since emerging from Texas in 2005 with the acclaimed Young Mountain EP, the group has built an international reputation for emotionally intense live performances and immersive albums including This Will Destroy You, Tunnel Blanket, and Another Language. Their music—often balancing delicate atmospheres with crushing crescendos—has been featured in major films and series (Moneyball, World War Z, Foxcatcher), cementing their status as one of the most influential voices in modern instrumental music.
