I HATE THE BOUNCERS is a punk band straight out of Berlin, formed in 2024. Founded by Giuseppe Arrivoli (guitar,bass & vocals) and Alessandro Emanuele (keys & drum machine), they began by blending punk,folk and alternative music with a signature LO-FI grit, fueled by their love for bir crusher pedals. Over time, the project evolved into a noisier, more aggressive and more synth-driven direction.Lyrics? Raw, real and never about love. Just life – with a sharp, hidden sense of humor.End of 2024, I HATE THE BOUNCERS released their first single "Wingspan", followed by their debut EP "Heute Nicht".In July 2025, Alessandro Emanuele left the project and Benedikt Hollerbach (drummer and producer) joined as an official member. The band finally swapped the drum machine for real drums while keeping the synth-driven vibe and adding distorted bass sound in their production."

Ghosts of Berlin: Joy and sorrow needs distortion We live under the curse of music. Our stage is the night. It makes us sometimes laugh, sometimes cry and through the streets of Berlin we wander in search of our motives... to jump, to scream, to shout, to be silent, to dance. We are the ghosts of Berlin. Silhouettes that get lost in horizons of prefabricated panels and concrete heroes, intoxicated by our favorite elixir: freedom. Toshìn & The Teleporters - 2 Sets!

I have had the pleasure of working with the power house artist Toshín,  several times since she has made her move to Berlin! I am really happy that we finally managed to get her own show here at one of my fave musical home, at Schokoladen! Toshín and her Band The Teleporters will be performing two sessions - so please do come out on time to catch both and we look forward to giving her the spotlight this coming July 15th she deserves!

Born in Nigeria, raised in Ireland, and now Berlin-based, Toshín is a powerful vocalist, songwriter, and performer whose music bridges soul, R&B, Afro-pop, jazz, and contemporary African influences. Renowned for her commanding stage presence and emotionally charged performances, she combines rich vocals, infectious rhythms, and compelling storytelling to create live experiences that are both uplifting and deeply human.

A prominent voice in Ireland’s evolving music landscape, Toshín explores themes of identity, belonging, resilience, joy, and empowerment through songs that draw from both her Nigerian heritage and Irish upbringing. Her performances effortlessly move between intimate reflection and exuberant celebration, inviting audiences into a space of connection, vulnerability, and collective energy. Equally captivating is her natural humor and warmth on stage, which create an immediate rapport with audiences and add an unexpected layer of charm and spontaneity to her performances.

She has appeared at major festivals including Electric Picnic and All Together Now, and has shared stages with artists such as Macy Gray, Gregory Porter, Madness, and Tank and the Bangas. She has even done a surprising duet with Bono at a Wedding! With her unique blend of musicality, charisma, wit, and authenticity, Toshín continues to establish herself as one of the most exciting emerging voices on the Irish and international scene.

Calamidades Lola (Barranquilla / Berlin📍) is a record hunter and dj/selector born and raised in Barranquilla, a city on the Caribbean coast of Colombia with a powerful culture of celebration and where music is a sure path to joy and liberation. She grew up surrounded by massive and colorful ‘picó’ sound systems that made the whole barrio vibrate with African and Caribbean rhythms. She currently lives in Berlin and much of her sound is influenced by her musical roots and her migratory experience.

die lesebühne

LSD - liebe statt drogen

Time
Einlass 19h Beginn 20h

Links

liebestattdrogen.de

Andreas „Spider“ Krenzke, Tobias „Tube“ Herre, Uli Hannemann, Eva Mirasol und Ivo Lotion präsentieren Geschichten, Lieder und Gäste.

LSD – Liebe Statt Drogen ist eine der bekanntesten Lesebühnen Berlins. Jeden Dienstagabend gestalten die 5 Mitglieder von LSD und eingeladene Gäste im Berliner Schokoladen ein Programm aus neuen Texten und Songs. Gegründet 1996 besteht LSD heute aus den Mitgliedern Mia Pittroff, Richard Blaha, Andreas „Spider“ Krenzke, Uli Hannemann, Tobias „Tube“ Herre und Ivo Smolak. Alle 5 genannten Künstler sind als Autoren und Musiker auch in anderen Bereichen aktiv.

LSD bietet jeden 1. & 3. Dienstag ein Offenes Mikro für Menschen an, die spontan etwas vortragen wollen. Beachtet bitte, dass der Beitrag selbstverfasst sein muss und nicht länger als 6 Minuten dauert. Wer am Offenen Mikro liest, braucht keinen Eintritt zahlen und bekommt ein Freigetränk.

Rotterdam-based artist LOVETH BESAMOH merges diverse influences from krautpunk and post-punk to afro-psychedelica and electronica into a sound that's as eclectic as it is compelling. With roots in Cameroon, where he honed his rhythmic talents in church and boarding school, Loveth seamlessly blends traditional African influences with modern genres to create music that's uniquely his own.

Initially a solo project, Loveth Besamoh evolved into a dynamic four-piece band. Featuring Ywan van der Keur (drums), Do Milder (guitar), Quincy Fortes (bass), and Loveth himself on vocals and djembe, their live shows are known for their electrifying energy.

—

APELAST is a psych-stoner rock band blending fuzzy, blues-infused solos with shoegaze textures and raw energy.

Fronted by vocalist, guitarist, and producer Neta Apelast (also known for work with Acid Boys, Maggot Heart, Lucifer, and Otherface), the band performs in various lineups—ranging from a stripped-down duo to a full four-piece ensemble.

Drawing inspiration from acts like Queens of the Stone Age, Uncle Acid & the Deadbeats, and Grand Funk Railroad, Apelast crafts a powerful sound that fuses high-octane rock'n'roll with emotional ballads. With gritty riffs, catchy melodies, and soulful vocals, their dynamic blend of heavy and heartfelt will shake your bones and stay in your head.
